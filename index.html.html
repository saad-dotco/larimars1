Larimar Oxford Shirt: A Blend of Timeless Elegance and Modern Comfort

The Legacy of the Oxford Shirt

The Oxford shirt, named after the durable cloth used to make it, has been a cornerstone of men’s wardrobes since the 19th century. Designed as a functional yet stylish option, it evolved into a symbol of understated sophistication. Its versatility allows it to transition effortlessly from casual to formal settings.
